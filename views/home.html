What is a simulation platform?

a SIMULATION is a technique of representing the real world with the virtual world.


the PLATFORM includes server hardware, a cloud operating system, & a suite of interconnected software tools.


Building industry stakeholders will be empowered to make informed decisions on how to reduce energy consumption in buildings.
